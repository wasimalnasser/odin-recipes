Fluffy Scrambled Eggs



Description

These fluffy scrambled eggs without milk will be your new favorite way to get that creamy, fluffy texture without needing a single splash of milk. Whether youâ€™re dairy-free, on a low-carb diet, or neither, these amazing eggs are the perfect breakfast to start the day.


Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp French butter (or olive oil)
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tbsp salsa macha
  • feta cheese
  • Everything Bagel Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Whisk four room-temperature eggs.
  2. Heat French butter (or olive oil) on a pan at medium-low heat. Then, add your eggs.
  3. Cook eggs on low heat, gently bringing the eggs back and forth using a spatula. Be patient here, it's worth it!
  4. Just when it looks almost done, turn off the heat, as it will continue cooking using its own temperature.
  5. Serve it up with optional toppings like sliced avocado, salsa macha, feta cheese and Everything Bagel Seasoning. Enjoy!
